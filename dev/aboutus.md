# About Jo & Roxy

I'm Jo, the son of Elaine and Jason and have been around Triumphs all my life.
I have "*driven*" Triumphs since I could sit on my parents lap and reach the steering wheel, but I first drove a Spitfire on the road in 2015.
![Jo sat on a spitfire](/assets/jovgt.webp "Jo sat on a spitfire"){width="50%" loading="lazy"}![my first time driving a spitfire](/assets/1stdrive.webp "my first time driving a spitfire"){width="50%" loading="lazy"}
I'm Roxy and whilst I didn't grow up around Triumphs, they have been a key part of Jo and I's relationship. When he took me to my first IOW TSSC weekend in 2013, I absolutely loved it! Since then I have enjoyed every adventure we have had in our Spitfire, including our road trip to Le Mans classic in 2018.
![driving at lemans](/assets/lemans.webp){width="100%" loading="lazy"}

In 2023 you would have seen us on the cover of The Courier for our wedding.
![Jo and Roxy in their spitfire on there wedding day](/assets/courier.webp "Jo and Roxy in their spitfire on there wedding day"){width="100%" loading="lazy"}

We have run the Isle of Wight TSSC Area for 3 years now and are looking forward to continuing the area and its events.
